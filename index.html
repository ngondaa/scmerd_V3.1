Tuesday, 28 October 2025

2025 SAIMechE Central Branch Conference on Mechanical Engineering and Related Disciplines

LEARN MORE SUBMIT TO CONFERENCE

Innovating Mechanical, Industrial and Aeronautical Engineering for a Sustainable Future

The conference will be held at the University of the Witwatersrand, Johannesburg, South Africa, with satellite online venues at other regional universities.

The conference welcomes original research or review manuscripts that have not been published or submitted elsewhere for possible presentation and publication.

CONFERENCE THEMES

Mechanical Design, Computational Simulations and Models, Solid and Fluid Mechanics, Mechatronics, Materials Design and Testing, Advanced Manufacturing Processes, Engine and Fuel Technology, Energy and Renewable Energy, Operations Research, Lean Manufacturing, Aerodynamics, Flight Dynamics

SCOPE OF CONFERENCE

This event will bring together postgraduate students, academics, and industry professionals on a platform that will showcase research in the fields of mechanical, industrial, and aeronautical engineering. We invite submissions of abstracts, extended abstracts, technical papers, and full conference papers aligned with the conference themes.

PUBLICATION

Abstracts, extended abstracts, and technical papers will be published in a conference proceedings after undergoing a development-oriented review process. The conference proceedings will be published on the Conference website. Accepted full conference papers will be published in a DHET accredited journal/conference proceedings

Submission Process

Submissions should be made electronically through this website.
  • The conference welcomes original research or review manuscripts that have not been published or submitted elsewhere for possible publication. Only manuscripts relevant to the conference themes will be accepted. Authors are advised to limit their papers to a maximum of 14 pages and a minimum of 10 pages.
  • All submitted manuscripts will undergo rigorous blind peer-review.
  • Templates:
    • Full conference paper(Engineering Proceedings option): Download
    • Full conference paper (EPJ option): Download

  • Accepted conference papers will be published in EPJ Web of Conferences (a DHET accredited proceedings/journal).
